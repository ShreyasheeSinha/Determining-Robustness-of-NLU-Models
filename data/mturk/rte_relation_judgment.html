Judgment of relationship between sentences

Instructions

Thank you for participating in this HIT! You will evaluate the relationship between two sentences. The goal is to improve comprehension of languages by computers: your assistance is crucial to building better technologies behind services like Amazon Alexa, Apple Siri, or Google Translate.

You will be presented with pairs of sentences. You would have to tell us the following by picking true or false: Assuming the first sentence is true, would the second sentence be likely true as well. 

How do I judge?

The following are some examples of the relationships between a pair of sentences.

  • Sentence 1: The president was assassinated.
  • Sentence 2: The president is dead.

     Given the first sentence is true, the second will be true as well.

                  true
                  false
  • Sentence 1: The president was assassinated.
  • Sentence 2: The president visited Los Angeles yesterday.

     Given the first sentence is true, the second will be true as well.

                  true
                  false

 

Notes

  • For sentences with ambiguous meanings, please try to infer as best you can. Some of them might have minor spelling mistakes or an extra word here or there. You can make a note in the comments section if you want.
